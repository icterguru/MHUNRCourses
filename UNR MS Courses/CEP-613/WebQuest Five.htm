WebQuest Five

Instructional Principles and Developing
Technology Integrated Lesson Plans

Introduction

This WebQuest will take you to a plethora of Web sites that will increase your understanding of some of the basic principles of lesson plan development and some of the practical implications of developing lesson plans that truly use technology to enhance teaching and learning. You will have an opportunity to evaluate some lesson plans and to design your own lesson plan.

Task

In this WebQuest, you will demonstrate an acquired understanding of some of the theoretical foundations that form good practice in teaching with information technology by developing and presenting an information technology enhanced lesson plan.

Conclusion

As you complete this WebQuest, you should have a broader and deeper understanding of some of the important principles relating to good technology enhanced lesson plans. This experience should prepare you to develop and execute lesson plans that make wise use of technology when you are in your own classroom.
