WebQuest Three

Elements of Project-Based Learning



Introduction

This WebQuest is about project-based learning. One of the key elements in the theoretical foundation of this theory relates to the effective use of information technology in the classroom. The purpose of this WebQuest is to assist you in becoming familiar with theories and practices relating to project-based learning. To demonstrate your knowledge of project-based learning you will develop a PowerPoint presentation that presents the key concepts of porject-based learning.

The Task

You have been given an assignment to make a presentation to your fellow teachers on Project-Based Learning. You will prepare a PowerPoint presentation that covers this topic and provides ways in which your fellow teachers can begin to implement project-based learning activities in their classrooms. You will share your PowerPoint presentation with your classmates.

Exercise B - Developing Your PowerPoint Presentation

PowerPoint Presentation Guidelines:
1. Demonstrate that you have a significant understanding of what project-based learning is.
2. Make the tone of the presentation useful to in-service teachers.
3. Present all concepts clearly and succinctly.
4. Offer practical suggestions and examples to make your points.
5. Offer suggestions on how a teacher could begin converting some lessons and assignments to fit into a project-based learning model.

Exercise C--Send Your PowerPoint Presentation to Your Instructor and Your Classmates
Use the Mail attachment function to attach a copy of your PowerPoint presentation to send to your instructor and to each of your classmates.
