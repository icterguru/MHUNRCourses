Web-Based Online Instruction


Introduction
Congratulations. You have successfully logged on to WebCampus and have located the first assignment (WebQuest) for this course. If you still need help to login click here: help options.

You are about to begin a Web-based online course. Just like a face-to-face classroom course, you will have classmates and an instructor. Although you may not see your classmates or your instructor in person, interaction with them is still important.

In this course you will deal with a teaching and learning technique called a WebQuest. You will use the technique in two (2) ways. First, you will complete the lessons and assignments in the course as WebQuests, structured in the WebQuest format. Second, you will create a WebQuest as a "teaching/learning plan" and post it on the Web. You will begin with this first WebQuest (WebQuest 1). This WebQuest will familiarize you with WebCampus, which is UNR's Web learning management system. This system is UNR's version of Blackboard, which is a natioonwide management system for electronic learning. You will also use some Internet communications tools, such as email and discussion.

 

The Task

Your task includes using the email program in WebCampus to receive and send several email documents. It also includes participating on the Discussion Board in WebCampus. Finally, you will create a Word document that contains your UNR NetID, an autobiography, and a digital image of yourself.

Process and Resources

Exercise A - WebCampus Introduction

You will not need to turn in anything for this exercise, but you will need to become familiar with using WebCampus
You will find a Tutorial on using WebCampus here.

Exercise B - Using WebCampus Private Mail

If you are not acquainted with how to use the mail program in WebCampus go to this Web site
When you are acquainted with how to use the mail program in WebCampus, complete the following steps:

1. Read the message from your instructor -- Instructor Message One
2. Reply to the message from your instructor. Use the Reply button to respond to the instructor message.
3. Create a message and send it to your instructor indicating what you hope to learn throughout the course.
4. Send a message to each of your classmates briefly introducing yourself to them.

Exercise C - Using the WebCampus Discussion Board
Go to the same Web site to become acquatinted with how to use the Discussion Board. Click on Discussions.

When you get to the Discussions section, complete the following steps:

1. Read the first topic under All from your instructor.
2. Reply to the question asked by your instructor.
3. Reply to a message sent by one of your classmates.
4. You are encouraged to keep the discussion going.

Exercise D - Create a Word Document Containing the Following Information:

1. Your NetID
When you became a UNR student, you received a NetID and a password. On your Word document list your NetID.
2. An autobiography

On this same Word document, write a brief autobiography page. Include the following:
1. Name
2. Place of birth
3. Present living location (Not your address, just the city and state)
4. Occupation--If you are a teacher, where do you teach?
5. A summary of your life's experiences (What are some of the interesting things that have happened to you?)
6. Hobbies/Interests
7. Your level of proficiency with computers and electronic communications devices.
8. A digital image of yourself--You can obtain an image in one of the following ways:
a. Have someone take your picture with a digital camera
b. Scan an image of yourself--there are scanners at many locations at the university
c. Have an image taken of yourself with a film camera and and have it processed in digital format
