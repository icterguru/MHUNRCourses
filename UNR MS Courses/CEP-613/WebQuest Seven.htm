WebQuest Seven

Training Teachers to Integrate Technology

Introduction

In this final WebQuest, you will use all the information you have gathered and the knowledge you have acquired to develop an in service training session for a group of practicing teachers.

Task

Since you are well trained in using technology to enhance teaching and learning, you have been asked to present a workshop for a group of fellow teachers on how to integrate technology into the classroom. The school district has assured you that this will be a model school and that, within reason, the necessary hardware, software, and connectivity will be made available. Your first task will be to prepare a PowerPoint presentation that will cover the first hour of the workshop. You will use the outline below as the guide for your presentation. serve as an introduction to the workshop. This well be the first presentation of the workshop and will introduce the participating teachers to the topic and provide an overview of what the workshop will cover.

Process

1. Presentation Outline--use this outline format to plan your presentation. Your presentation should include at least 12 PowerPoint Slides.

Format Format for Workshop Presentation

  1. Guiding principles for integrating technology into the classroom
  2. Basic changes in teaching philosophy
  3. Guidelines for classroom management
  4. Guidelines for the curriculum
  5. The role of the teacher
  6. The classroom configuration
  7. Some of the benefits for developing an integrated technology classroom

2. The Web sites in the Resources section below will provide a starting point for you to develop your PowerPoint presentation, you will need to do some Web searching of your own.
3. When you have completed your PowerPoint slides for this presentation, attach the presentation to a message to your instructor.

Conclusion

In this WebQuest you have had an opportunity to think through a model classroom that integrates information technology into the curriculum. The sky was the limit and you should have had fun creating this learning environment without restrictions. The real world will obviously not be this way, but this experience will help you know what you want and help you request useful resources when and if the time comes that you do have the opportunity to add to your classroom technology base.
