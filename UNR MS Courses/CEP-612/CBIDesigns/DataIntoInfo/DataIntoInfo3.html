Converting Data into Information

 

Information Processing Cycle (IPC):

 

The process of conversion of data into information is well known as- Information Processing Cycle (IPC).

 

The process IPC includes four basic steps:

 

   1)   Input,

   2)   Processing,

   3)   Storing/Restring, and

   4)   Output.
